Master's Thesis

My master's thesis is titled An Exploration of Swedishness: A Mosaic of the Swedish Cultural Landscape Through the Lens of Children's Film. This thesis aspired to discuss the role of Children’s films in the dissemination of ideas, idylls, values, and norms associated with the vague cultural idea that is Swedishness. It looks at two contrasting children’s films that represent two sides of the same coin. Through modes of film analyses this thesis explores two films and two separate frameworks of important national concepts, the landscape, and the individual. The analysis sheds light on how inherent Swedish idylls and ideals, blend through cultural productions and become perhaps not as distinct or idyllic as they may seem. Link

Argentina, the Perons, and Children's Books

This research project focused on the relationship between children and the state apparatus. The case of Argentina is particularly interesting as scholars have had an increasing interest in children being an influential component to the success of Juan and Eva Peron and Peron's almost cultish following, the Peronistas (Peronists) This paper uses socio-historic and visual analysis to discuss the power of children's media as a tool within populist rhetoric.
